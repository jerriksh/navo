If the responsive part is not implemented, the navigation bar will not adapt to smaller screens, and all navigation links will continue to display as they would on larger screens. This could lead to usability issues on mobile devices or screens with limited width, as the navigation links may not fit or may become difficult to interact with. By including the responsive part in the code, the navigation bar is designed to be more user-friendly on smaller screens. The navigation links are adjusted to be more suitable for the limited space, making it easier for users to navigate on devices with smaller viewports.
