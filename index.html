About JNV Seraikela The 15th Nov, 1986 was the most auspicious day when the foundation stone of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sijulata, Seraikela was laid down by Hon IAS Smt. Rajbala Veerma present Deputy commissioner of Seraikela district, Jharkhand. The school campus covers around 33 acres of forested hillside with its buildings connected by steep and windy paths and offering spectacular views at every turn. These buildings include the beautiful old tree and newly constructed infrastructure and the newly-refurbished student hostels and staff houses. The academic buildings including several classrooms are linked by a computer network. The widespread use of email and an intranet system shrinks the distances and keeps staff, students and administration in quick and easy contact with one another. The Vidyalaya is surrounded by the grove of cashew trees beside main Tata-Chaibasa National Highway (NH-88), at Sijulata, Seraikela, Jharkhand.
